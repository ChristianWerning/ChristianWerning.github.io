Peer Instruction
for very large Groups

PINGO is a free live-feedback system for the active involvement of students in taught lessons. Particularly suited to teaching in large lecture halls.

Register as a lecturer learn more ›
{% include nav.html %}

No installation – no additional hardware required.

PINGO operates via your browser. It is made available for free by the University of Paderborn. Students can participate simply by accessing a browser with their smartphone, tablet or notebook. Lecturers also use a browser to launch or close a survey, and to prepare their questions.

Start immediately

You can start using PINGO straight after registering. No preparation is required. Launch your first survey directly as a quick-session.

Over 1300 lecturers already use PINGO
